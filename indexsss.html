About Me

Hello and welcome. Where do I start? I am a full-time front-end web developer at a small tech company in Austin, TX. I’ve been a front-end web dude for about 6 years now. I love every minute of it. I also dabble in a few other things some would call hobbies. I’ve been running a clothing line, Timber Threads for a little over 5 years now. It started by creating some abstract art in Photoshop, then I decided to slap them on a t-shirt. I also co-run a hockey company, Timber Hockey on the side with a partner.

So this brings me to another hobby of mine, photography. I’ve been behind the camera snapping pics for close to 3 years now. My interest came when I moved from Denver to Idaho for a job. All my photographer contacts that I had made shooting stuff for my clothing brand had all been in Denver. While attending the shoots I found that I really enjoyed being involved and took interest in it quickly, so I decided to pick up a camera and give it a try. I fell in love with it. There is something intangible about creating art with an image that drives me to keep shooting, something raw and fulfilling. The high I get when a photo gets people to react in anyway, is something that never gets old.
